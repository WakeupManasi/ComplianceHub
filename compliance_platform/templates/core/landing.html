How the Agentic AI Pipeline Works

Our 7-agent autonomous swarm continuously monitors, analyses, and reports on threats without human intervention.

1

Orchestrator Deploys

Master agent coordinates the pipeline, assigns tasks to specialised agents based on priority and threat type.

2

Agents Scan & Crawl

Recon Agent, DarkWeb Crawler, and Email Hunter autonomously scan dark web forums, breach databases, and Telegram channels.

3

AI Analyses Threats

Threat Analyser uses NLP to classify severity. IOC Extractor identifies malicious IPs, domains, and file hashes.

4

Reports & Alerts

Report Generator compiles executive briefs. Alerts are sent to organiser email and SOC team with actionable recommendations.
